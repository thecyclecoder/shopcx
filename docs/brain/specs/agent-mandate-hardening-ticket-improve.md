# Harden the Ticket Improve agent — roll persistent coaching into its mandate

**Owner:** [[../functions/platform]] · **Parent:** [[platform-director-agent]] — graduate persistent coaching into a durable fix (director grades agents: low score → fix spec, never a CEO escalation)
**Found in use 2026-07-01:** the **Ticket Improve** agent (`ticket-improve`) sits at **6.1/10** after **2** coaching attempts that didn't stick. Rather than escalate to the CEO, roll the coaching into a permanent fix so the agent improves at the mandate level.

## The accumulated coaching to bake in (now archived as rolled-into-mandates)
- **When Every low-graded turn closes with prose diagnosis (or an open 'which do you want?' question) but omits the mandated change_summary envelope — all 10 jobs show pending_actions:[] and a log_tail that is only raw final-message JSON + usage metadata, giving the grader no before/after, no field/tag delta, and no customer-voice quote to score.:** Before you close ANY ticket-improve turn, self-check that your final JSON carries the change_summary envelope: text_diff {before,after}, field_changes with old→new, tags_added/removed, and customer_voice with a quoted key phrase from the customer (or no_changes:true + a one-line note on a pure-investigation turn). And when the founder asked you to DO something (send the journey, escalate, link accounts, close the ticket), do NOT narrate 'Two options: (A)/(B)… which do you want?' — emit status:'propose' with a typed plan.actions (e.g. an orchestrator_action for the journey) so the executor can act on one approval. A grader can only score what it can observe; a completed turn with no diff is indistinguishable from a no-op. — SKILL.md:57-60 makes change_summary MANDATORY and states its absence 'caps the rubric at ~6/10 regardless of how correct the underlying work was,' with SKILL.md:96 rendering the missing block as a grader flag; job 5d7209a0's log_tail ends literally '…Which do you want?' as a status:reply with pending_actions:[] (grade 4), and the eight 6/10s all have pending_actions:[] plus a metadata-only log_tail — exactly the visibility gap SKILL.md:50,53 (status:'propose' + smallest correct plan) and the change_summary mandate exist to close.
- **When Tilly consistently completes ticket-improve jobs without producing a visible before/after diff or change summary, making it impossible for graders to verify that the ticket genuinely improved, customer voice was preserved, and no meaning was changed.:** When you finish improving a ticket, always emit a structured change summary as your final output — showing the original field values alongside the revised values (title, description, category, tags), plus a one-sentence rationale for each change — before marking the job completed. — Graders cannot reward work they cannot inspect; every low grade explicitly cites the absence of a diff or rationale as the reason they cannot confirm rubric criteria were met. A visible before/after record also forces Tilly to self-check that customer voice and meaning were actually preserved before closing the job.

## Phase 1 — bake the coaching into the agent
- Make the above coaching PERMANENT behavior of the `ticket-improve` agent — fold it into its prompt/mandate/code (its run-job + prompt in `scripts/builder-worker.ts` and the relevant `src/lib/agents/*`), not ephemeral appended `agent_instructions`. Once baked, the agent should follow it by default.
- Verify the agent's grade rollup recovers (≥ 7/10) over the next window of graded actions; if a coaching class still recurs, the bake-in missed it.
