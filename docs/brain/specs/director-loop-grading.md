# Director continuous loop + grading

**Owner:** [[../functions/platform]] ¬∑ **Parent:** M5 ‚Äî Continuous loop + grading
**Blocked-by:** [[platform-director-agent]]

The supervisory loop that closes the **CEO ‚Üí Director ‚Üí tool** chain for the [[../goals/devops-director]] goal. The [[platform-director-agent|Platform/DevOps Director]] runs on a **standing cadence** (a scheduled pass), and the CEO **grades the director's calls 1‚Äì10** ‚Äî *was the auto-approval right? did the escorted goal land clean?* ‚Äî and those grades **train it and tighten/loosen the leash**. This directly mirrors the shipped [[storefront-campaign-grading-loop|Head-of-Growth campaign-grading loop]] (an AI/human grader against a rubric + human-approved calibration rules, human-overridable, feeding back as a training signal) ‚Äî reusing its proven shape one level up the org chart. Today the leash in the goal is **static**: the [[platform-director-agent|M4 director]] auto-approves within a fixed policy with no feedback loop that widens or narrows the [[approval-routing-engine|`live + autonomous`]] envelope based on whether its past calls were good. Success metric served: **% of platform approvals the CEO never touches** can *safely* trend up because the director's decision quality is measured and the autonomy envelope is earned, not assumed.

## Phase 4 ‚Äî feed grades back to tighten/loosen the leash
- ‚úÖ shipped
- Grades **train the director**: a sustained high grade in a category widens its autonomy envelope (the CEO can promote a previously-escalated low-risk category into the auto-approve leash); a low grade narrows it (a category reverts to CEO-gated). The recommendation surfaces as an owner-confirmed change to Platform's [[approval-routing-engine|`live + autonomous`]] / leash policy ‚Äî **the CEO disposes; the loop never widens its own envelope unilaterally** ([[../operational-rules]] ¬ß North star).
- Surface per-period grades + trend + the leash-adjustment recommendations on the M1 Agents hub / M3 board (the CEO's report contract for the director).

**Shipped (code-complete, tsc-clean; depends on the Phase-2 migration being applied):** the read-back half of the loop ‚Äî grades in, owner-confirmed recommendations out, the director never self-promoting:
- `src/lib/agents/director-leash-recommendations.ts` ([[../libraries/director-leash-recommendations]]) `computeDirectorGradeReport({ workspaceId })` ‚Äî pure read over [[../tables/director_decision_grades]]: per-**dimension** (`auto-approvalÔΩúgoal-escort`) + per-**leash-category** (`error_fixÔΩúdb_healthÔΩúadditive_migrationÔΩúmonitoring_fix`, resolved by joining grade ‚Üí [[../tables/approval_decisions]] ‚Üí [[../tables/agent_jobs]]`.pending_actions`) stats with a **recent-vs-prior trend**, the actionable **leash-adjustment recommendations** (a scope with `‚â•3` graded calls + `avg ‚â•8` and not declining ‚Üí **loosen**; `avg ‚â§4.5` ‚Üí **tighten** ‚Äî "hold" omitted), the recent grade rows, the proposed [[../tables/director_grader_prompts]] rules, and the **current Platform [[../tables/function_autonomy]] envelope** (so the report shows state vs. recommendation). One level up the org chart from the campaign loop's `loadLeverGradeSignal`.
- **Recommend-only ‚Äî the CEO disposes** ([[../operational-rules]] ¬ß North star): the module has **no apply path** and never writes `function_autonomy`. The actual envelope change is the owner toggling **Autonomy** on the Agents hub (the existing `POST /api/developer/agents/autonomy`); the recommendation carries the current envelope + the rationale so the CEO can act. The loop can never widen its own leash.
- **Report surface** ‚Äî `GET /api/developer/agents/grades` (owner-gated) ‚Üí a new **Director grades** tab on the M1 Agents hub (CEO + Platform roles): the recommendations, per-dimension/category grades + trend, the proposed calibration rules (Approve/Reject), and the recent grades with a **one-click override**.
- **Human-overridable + calibrated** ‚Äî `POST /api/developer/agents/grades/{gradeId}` records `graded_by='human'` + `overridden_by` (the grader never re-writes a human grade); a ‚â•3-point override gap (or an explicit request) drafts a `proposed` [[../tables/director_grader_prompts]] rule (Opus). `PATCH /api/developer/agents/grader-prompts/{ruleId}` approves/rejects it ‚Äî only an `approved` rule reaches the grader ([[../libraries/director-grader]] `buildDirectorGraderSystemPrompt`), closing the calibration loop.

## Safety / invariants
- **Decision graded on soundness, not luck.** A sound auto-approval that later needed a (rare, reversible) tweak still grades well if the *reasoning* was right; a careless approval that happened to be fine grades low ‚Äî mirror [[storefront-campaign-grading-loop]]'s hypothesis-vs-result separation.
- **Human-overridable + calibrated.** Every grade can be overridden by the CEO; overrides record (`graded_by`/`overridden_by`) and become calibration rules ‚Äî never silently lost.
- **The leash widens only by the CEO.** Grades *recommend* tightening/loosening; the actual `live + autonomous` / leash change is an owner-confirmed action ‚Äî the director never expands its own authority ([[../operational-rules]] ¬ß North star).
- **Idempotent grading.** A decision is graded once per dimension; a re-run updates in place, never duplicates.
- **The grader is a supervised tool.** It scores a bounded proxy (decision quality); the CEO owns the objective and overrides it.
