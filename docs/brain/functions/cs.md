# CS Manager (function)

The permanent owner of **customer service quality** — ticket volume, CSAT, refund/return rate, and the AI's own performance as a support agent. One of the org-chart functions ([[../goals/ceo-mode]]); this doc is both the **CS-Manager director-agent's CEO-mode charter** and the **home that owns every CS mandate + spec**. A function is never "done" — it carries perpetual mandates and contributes to finite goals.

## Scope + owned metrics

- **Owns:** ticket volume + resolution, CSAT, refund/return rate, churn driven by support, and the AI agent's own answer quality.
- **North-star metrics:** CSAT, first-contact resolution, AI handle-rate vs. escalation, refund/return rate.
- **Data we have:** tickets, AI analyses, CSAT — already rich (this is ShopCX's original surface).
- **Gaps:** — (the data here is the most complete of any director; the gap is synthesis into the director report contract).
