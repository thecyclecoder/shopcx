# CFO (function)

The permanent owner of **the company's financials** — P&L, margin, cash, unit economics, LTV:CAC. One of the org-chart functions ([[../goals/ceo-mode]]); this doc is both the **CFO director-agent's CEO-mode charter** and the **home that owns every CFO mandate + spec**. A function is never "done" — it carries perpetual mandates and contributes to finite goals.

## Scope + owned metrics

- **Owns:** unified P&L, contribution margin, cash position, LTV:CAC, unit economics, the books.
- **North-star metrics:** monthly profit vs. the profitability floor, blended LTV:CAC, contribution margin %, enterprise-value proxy (EBITDA × multiple).
- **Data we have:** Shopify orders ([[../tables/orders]]), Braintree ([[../integrations/braintree-customer]]), Avalara tax. Order-level revenue, yes.
- **Gaps:** COGS / landed cost (margin isn't computable without it), a unified **metrics spine** so the analysts read one trusted source instead of 8 live APIs. Both surface from [[../goals/ceo-mode]] M1.

## Mandates (perpetual)

### Profitability & unit economics
Keep the company above its monthly-profit floor and drive contribution margin + LTV:CAC the right way — the bottom-line guardrail every other function spends against.
- **Metric:** monthly profit vs. floor, contribution margin %, blended LTV:CAC.
